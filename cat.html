All About Cats!

The personality of a cat differs from one cat to another. Some cats are friendly and love humans,while others don't like humans and want to live outside. A very rare cat breed is the cinnamon brown cat. Those cats are one of the most beautiful breed. Some other very rare cat breeds are the LaPerm, American Wirehair, Devon Rex, American Bobtail, Minskin, Sphynx,etc. Cats were on this earth for a very long time. The oldest cat breed in the world is the Egyptian Mau. It is believed that Egyptian Mau ancestors have been found mummified alongside Pharaohs in their tombs.

In ancient egypt, cats were not seen as mere animals. People thought they were magical because they are so powerful. It was believed that they bring good luck to the people who housed them in Ancient Egypt. To honor the cats, the wealthy people dressed them in jewels and gave them treats that were for royalty. When the cats died, they were mummified, and sometimes with the pharaohs. Cats in Ancient Egypt symbolised the power of the Gods. They even believed that one of them was a goddess of lower Egypt, she was protective and could bring great prosperity.
